

# SFIT Website Redesign – 2025 Modern University Standard

## Design Vision
A minimalist, typography-forward design that leads with SFIT's impressive achievements (NAAC A+, NBA accreditation) while maintaining elegant whitespace and subtle micro-animations. Think MIT meets Stanford – clean, confident, and prestigious.

---

## Color Palette (New Modern University Standard)
- **Primary**: Deep Navy Blue (#0F172A) – authority & trust
- **Accent**: Teal (#0D9488) – modern, tech-forward
- **Warm Accent**: Amber (#F59E0B) – for CTAs and highlights
- **Neutrals**: Slate grays for text hierarchy
- **Background**: Off-white (#FAFAFA) with subtle texture

---

## Sections Overview

### 1. Utility Bar (Top)
- Slim, subtle bar with contact info and quick links
- Social icons in monochrome, colorize on hover
- Staff/Student/Parent login dropdowns

### 2. Main Header
- SFIT logo with clean typography
- Accreditation badges displayed as minimal icons (not cluttered text)
- Sticky behavior on scroll

### 3. Navigation
- Clean horizontal navigation
- Dropdown menus on hover with smooth fade animation
- Mobile: Full-screen overlay menu with elegant transitions

### 4. Announcement Ticker
- Subtle animated banner below nav
- Single announcement at a time with gentle slide
- Dismiss option for users

### 5. Hero Section – "Achievement First"
- Large headline: "NAAC A+ Accredited • NBA Approved"
- Sub-headline: "Shaping Engineers Since 1999"
- Background: Placeholder for campus hero image
- Two CTAs: "Apply Now" (primary) & "Explore Programs" (secondary)
- Animated stats counters: Students, Faculty, Placements %

### 6. About SFIT
- Two-column layout: Text left, image placeholder right
- Key facts as elegant bullet points
- "Read More" link with arrow animation

### 7. Programs Offered
- Interactive accordion with smooth expand/collapse
- B.Tech programs with seat counts
- M.Tech and Ph.D. expandable sections
- Clean iconography for each department

### 8. Vision, Mission & Quality Policy
- Three elegant cards in a row
- Subtle hover lift effect
- Icon + headline + text format

### 9. News & Events
- Tab interface: "Notices" | "Events"
- Clean list layout with date badges
- "View All" links for each section

### 10. Why SFIT
- Dark gradient background section
- Four feature cards with icons
- NBA Accredited, High Placements, Infrastructure, Faculty
- Subtle parallax on background image

### 11. Our Recruiters
- Grayscale logo grid
- Color reveal on hover
- Infinite scroll marquee animation option

### 12. Inspired Learning
- Image grid/slider showcasing labs
- RADLAB, Robotics, hands-on learning
- Placeholder-ready for your photos

### 13. Testimonials
- Carousel with profile images
- Auto-slide with manual controls
- Quote, name, and role display

### 14. Footer
- Three columns: Contact, Quick Links, Connect
- Embedded Google Map placeholder
- Social icons
- Copyright and policy links

## Interactive Features
- Micro-animations on scroll (fade-in, slide-up)
- Hover effects on cards and buttons
- Smooth scroll behavior
- Scroll-to-top button
- WhatsApp floating button
- Mobile hamburger menu with full-screen overlay

---

## Responsive Design
- Desktop-first with tablet and mobile breakpoints
- Touch-friendly interactions
- No horizontal scroll
- Optimized image containers
